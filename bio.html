Richard, known to some as Rafa, is a seasoned leader with over a decade in the creative industry, including six years spearheading web design initiatives. He's this unique blend of independent thinking and practical adaptability, valuing both analog depth and digital innovation, always asking questions and on the hunt for timeless solutions and pushing for continuous growth. His early immersion in rapidly evolving technology, stemming from his 90s upbringing, fundamentally shaped his strategic perspective and ability to drive change in dynamic digital landscapes.

His broad range of interests, from sports and photography to physical music collections (yes, actual physical formats!) and digital playlist curation – and now a growing passion for motorcycling – speaks to a well-rounded individual who understands diverse consumer touchpoints and creative engagement. This multifaceted perspective allows him to connect seemingly disparate elements, fostering holistic solutions and anticipating customer needs. He's not just a doer; he's a strategic mind who can leverage varied insights to drive business objectives while learning from the best.

After a focused effort to earn his Graphic Design degree from Jorge Tadeo Lozano University in 2014, Richard immediately began building a robust foundation of real-world experience. His early ventures, including managing website content and providing exhibition support at the Valenzuela Klenner Art Gallery from 2009, quickly evolved into a practical understanding of digital and print production. This hands-on background, combined with his continuous learning mindset, positions him as a visionary leader ready to guide teams and organizations through complex digital transformations.

Interests: sports, photography and books, music collection in physical formats or playlist creations, and motorcycling.
